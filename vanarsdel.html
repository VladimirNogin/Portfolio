Van Arsdel Lights

Overview

Van Arsdel Lights was a showcase app of the year for Build 2018 that encompassed many new features and highlighted existing tools developers can leverage and create amazing apps for the Windows Store. Our main goal was to capture a larger developer community and help drive store engagement. I approached this challenge the way I do for all my projects by first asking important questions to stakeholders and the development team that will heavily shape the product and experience we will design.
