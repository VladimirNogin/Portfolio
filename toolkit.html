Creating presets

Windows Community Toolkit is a Fluent UWP app that I’ve helped drive to success from it’s beginning. This app aims to hit many business and user requirements some being community engagement, growing UWP dev base and helping new Windows platform developers learn XAML.

Adding value

I wanted the app to not only provide usefull code snippets but also be a place where designer and developers can learn about XAML and the Microsoft design language.

Usability studies

My team and I kept a close relationship to our user base which constantly provided us with fantastic feedback that we then integrated into our usability studies. We preformed usability studies on existing and new features to gave us incredible insight into what was working and what needed to be improved. All this effort resulted in positive feedback from our community.
