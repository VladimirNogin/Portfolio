Storyteller

Project overview

Storyteller is a video capture/editor for iOS, created for users to express their creativity, capture those special moments and tell their story their way. Storyteller is a standalone app that could be connected to hardware via bluetooth. Everyone has a story to tell so I set out to create an app that can empower users to tell their story their way.
