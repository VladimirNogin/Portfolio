Society Grl

Project overview

Society grl is a women's fashion blog aimed to promote products, events and latest in style. Branding and clean design was crucial to the success of this project.

Sketching & wireframing

I wanted my customers vision of a beautiful and clean design come true, that is why I listened carefully and included my cusomter in my sketching/wireframing process.

The previous website

Below is a shot of the old website for comparison. One of the biggest problem I found when taking a inventory of the current website was that it wasn't mobile friendly, actually it wasn't responsive at all. Along with creating a clean navigational hierarchy I also implemented strong branding across the website, witch was lacking before.

Overall I'm glad to report that the client was very pleased with the outcome.
