Professional experience

Hi, my name is Vlad and I'm a UI/UX Product Designer. As a product designer with over 4 years of experience I have crafted responsive & engaging user experiences across a wide range of digital platforms. My design proccess

I believe listening and asking the right questions early on in a product design cycle is important, matter of fact I think it's very important. I begin by sitting down with stakeholders and gathering as much product requirements as possible. I then dig deep into the market and users I would be designing for, I do this by talking to them and conducting usability studies like i've done for my past projects that I've displayed here. Aligning business requirements along with user/customer expectations will lead to a better and successful product in the end. All this is followed by wirefrmaing, prototyping, design and then a back and forth process with developers on brining my designs and ideas to life.
